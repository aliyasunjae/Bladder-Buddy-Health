📘 Bladder Health Guide

💡 What is Bladder Health?
Bladder health means your urinary system is working well to:
  • ✔ Store urine properly
  • ✔ Empty regularly
  • ✔ Prevent infections
⚠️ Common Problems (LUTS)
  • 🔥 Pain when urinating
  • 🚨 Urgency
  • 💧 Frequent urination
  • 🦠 Urinary Track Infection
🚫 Why Adolescents at Risk?
  • ❌ Not drinking enough water
  • ❌ Holding urine in class
  • ❌ Poor hygiene
💧 Healthy Tips
  • ✔ Drink 6–8 glasses
  • ✔ Don’t hold urine
  • ✔ Go every 3–4 hours
  • ✔ Stay clean & dry
